Chester Stevenson, MEd

CEO & Founder

Chester Stevenson is an instructional designer with more than twelve years experience supporting instructional design and training for smaller companies, government, and now the Boeing company. Chester has developed and delivered blended projects for classroom, webinar, and eLearning. He has served the foundation in a variety of capacities, including instructional design, project management and performance improvement. Chester has a Bachelors of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Human Resources and Management. He also holds a Masters of Science in Instructional and Performance Technology and a Masters Certificate in Workplace eLearning and Performance Support from Boise State University.
