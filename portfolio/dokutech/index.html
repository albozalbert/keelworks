Dokutech

 

DOKU:TECH explores and challenges the social implications of technological innovations through connectivity, content, openness and security. Taking place in the beautiful city of Prizren, Kosovo the event gathers reputable speakers, observers, explorers and builders from around the world to talk about open knowledge, digital security, privacy, social entrepreneurship and business.

Appsix was one of the partners of the event involved in the design and development of the applicationÂ for both iOS and Android, basing all inspiration for the application in the event branding. The application features a minimal approach in the design focusing on the elements of the schedule, speakers and organiser details. The user has the ability to browse through the conference speakers, schedule and workshop details and also other related information.

DOKU:TECH is organised by IPKO Foundation, SHARE Foundation and DokuFest.

Project Date: August 2014.
